## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- ✅ A BoxLog account ([sign up for free](https://boxlog.io/signup))
- ✅ Your preferred programming language installed
- ✅ Basic knowledge of your application's architecture
- ✅ Admin access to your development environment
